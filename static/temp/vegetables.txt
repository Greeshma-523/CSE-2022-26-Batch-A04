Project Title: Reusable Platform
Modules:
User:
Register: Users can register by entering their details.
Login: Users can log in after being approved by the admin.
View All Products: Users can view all available products.
Upload Products: Users can upload products to the platform.

Collect Product: Users can collect the products.
Chat with Users: Users can chat with each other.
View User Profiles: Users can view their own profiles and other users' profiles.
Admin:
Login: Admins can log in to the website.
View and Allow Users: Admins can view and approve users on the website.
View Products: Admins can view the uploaded products.
View Collected Products: Admins can view the collected products.
Estimated Time: 50-60 hours
